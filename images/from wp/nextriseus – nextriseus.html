OUR SERVICES

Explore Our Services

The company's current flagship product is solar tracking TRACK accessories. The photovoltaic support product line covers the core components of photovoltaic support such as TTU, sandalwood, stamping parts RAIL and robot welding parts BHA. Products are divided into major categories and a total of subcategories. The company has a mature technical development team, which can provide customized services according to customer needs, closely cooperate with customer technology upgrades, and provide supporting production support. The company controls each processing and production link, and provides three-guarantee services for the products produced according to industry standards. The company's related products have been widely used in photovoltaic ground power stations, distributed power stations and other fields, and have won high recognition from the market and customers. At the same time, with good mold development, maintenance and maintenance capabilities, as well as product technology innovation and production quality management capabilities, the company continues to drive, transform, and upgrade the overall product business layout to ensure the company's competitive advantage.
