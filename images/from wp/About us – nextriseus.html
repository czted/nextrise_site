Company Profile

CZT (USA) INC. was established on October 7, 2022. It is an enterprise dedicated to the research and development and production of new energy equipment accessories. With the advancement of science and technology, the development of technology and the exhaustion of global resources, various countries advocate the development and utilization of new energy. In particular, solar power generation, as an important part of new energy development, has made great progress since the 1970s and has made great progress in terms of energy ratio and technological innovation. With the layout and demand of the global market, on October 7, 2022, CZT (USA) INC. was established in the United States to build a localized team in the United States to develop and produce solar tracking TRACK systems and other new energy equipment related accessories to serve the United States. New energy equipment manufacturer.CZT ENERGY (USA) INC. has passed ISO9001, ISO14001, IECQC080000, OHSAS 18001, TS 16949, and UL Certification of the United states. It will be totally 500 employees.

Products and services

The company’s current flagship product is solar tracking TRACK accessories. The photovoltaic support product line covers the core components of photovoltaic support such as TTU, sandalwood, stamping parts RAIL and robot welding parts BHA. Products are divided into major categories and a total of subcategories. The company has a mature technical development team, which can provide customized services according to customer needs, closely cooperate with customer technology upgrades, and provide supporting production support. The company controls each processing and production link, and provides three-guarantee services for the products produced according to industry standards. The company’s related products have been widely used in photovoltaic ground power stations, distributed power stations and other fields, and have won high recognition from the market and customers.
